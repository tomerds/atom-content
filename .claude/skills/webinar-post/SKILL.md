# Webinar Post

## Before writing

1. Read every example in `examples/webinar-posts/` — match their voice, structure, and length. The examples are the source of truth.
2. Confirm the essentials before drafting: webinar title, date/time (with timezone), speakers (names, titles, orgs), registration or recording link, and the 2–3 key takeaways. Ask for anything missing.

## Process

1. Create a folder at the repo root: `<Webinar_Name>_Webinar_Post/`.
2. Draft in `draft.md` inside that folder.
3. Audience: research administrators and university research office staff, unless told otherwise.
